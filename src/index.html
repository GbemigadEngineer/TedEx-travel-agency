Who Are We?

TEDEX CONSULTING LIMITED is a premier travel agency and global mobility consultancy specializing in providing sponsored travel opportunities for individuals seeking to study and work abroad. Established in Lagos, Nigeria, the company offers comprehensive visa processing services and travel solutions to destinations worldwide.

With a focus on facilitating international education and career advancement, TEDEX CONSULTING LIMITED serves as a bridge connecting ambitious individuals with life-changing opportunities abroad. The company's expertise lies in navigating complex visa processes, securing Certificates of Sponsorship (COS), and creating reliable travel routes for clients.

Our Vision

To be the premier global mobility facilitator, transforming lives through seamless international education and career opportunities.

Our Mission

At TEDEX CONSULTING LIMITED, we are committed to connecting ambitious individuals with life-changing international opportunities through expert visa consulting, personalized travel solutions, and dedicated sponsorship services.
